Android Dev Summit 2015

  • Thu, Nov 26, 2015
  • One-minute read

On November 23rd and 24th there has been the annual Android Dev Summit from the Computer History Museum in Mountain View.

Over 16 hours of content across two days that are now available on the Android Developers YouTube channel.

Here is the opening Keynote by Dave Burke, VP of Android Engineering, and Stephanie Cuthbertson, the GPM for Android Studio.

Here is the schedule of the two days event.

Day 1: Framework Skills

  • Android Application Architecture
  • Respecting User Attention: Notification Best Practices
  • Secrets to App & Game Success on Google Play
  • Keep it Secret, Keep it Safe
  • Android Textual Layout
  • RecyclerView Animations and Behind the Scenes
  • Support Library: Guts and Glory
  • “Mother, May I?” Asking for Permissions
  • Framework Fireside Chat

Day 2: Tooling and Performance

  • What’s New in Android Studio
  • Data Binding – Write Apps Faster
  • User Identity and Sign In with Google
  • The Android Build System
  • App Standby vs. Doze: Understanding System Sleep
  • Android Testing
  • State of Media Playback
  • Android Studio for Experts
  • Developing for Android: the Naughty Bits
  • Android Tools and Testing Fireside Chat
